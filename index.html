Frequently Asked Questions

WHAT IS THE PRICE OF GAME?

While we can't say anything definite about the price, we want you to know that we have to act according to whatever minimum limit Steam gives us. For this reason, we will try to arrange it in a way that does not upset the Turkish player as much as possible.

WHEN WILL THE GAME BE OUT?

There are those who have heard from the publications that it will be released in 2023. But we want you to know that the game will be released as 'EARLY ACCESS' when it comes out.

WILL THE GAME HAVE A STORY?

It's a survival game.

CAN IT BE PLAYED EVERY COMPUTER

Our work is in this direction. Optimization is one of our team's priorities. We don't want you to have any questions about this issue.

DO YOU HAVE OFFICIAL ACCOUNTS?

Instagram : instagram.com/rygradofficial/
Twitter : twitter.com/Rygradofficial

WHAT IS RYGRAD NEWS

It is a weekly or daily newspaper published by volunteers in our Discord. (Perhaps your name may be in that newspaper one day.)

WILL THERE BE OTHER GAMES IN THE FUTURE?

Yes, our plans are in that direction, but as you say, 'in the future'.

WILL THERE BE STEAM WORKSHOP AND SKIN SALES?

Workshop items and modding the game etc. We will have studies like this, but we do not know if it will reach early access. We are not able to give any information about the sale of skins at the moment.

WILL THERE BE UNDERWATER LIFE AND BOSSES?

Boss additions will be made to the game, but we want you to know that it is in the planning stage, although it is not certain. We cannot give precise information about underwater life.

WILL IT BE A SINGLE MAP?

No, we will have a few maps randomly and you will be able to choose them or play them randomly.

WILL THE GAME HAVE A WEAPON?

Since our game will be in the Medieval (Medieval) theme, there will be no firearms. Instead, there will be many medieval-themed weapons such as Bow - Crossbow - Scorpion - Catapult.

HOW WILL THE SHIP SYSTEM AND THE NAVAL WAR?

You will be able to create your ship with the level system, a level 1 raft is like a level 3 ship. You will be able to attack and defend with a weapon that you will add to your ship in naval wars.
